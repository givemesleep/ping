My father's name is Roderick dela Cruz Mabutin working at private company, he collects receipts in a day and made a sideline as a Angkas Driver at night for extra income. Joshua Allen Galang Mabutin is my brother's name and he is currently studying at St. Claire College at Almar, Zabarte, a graduating Senior High School student. I was born in a very religous family surrounded by people who truly love her and spoils her with the word of God. Due to my health condition, my parents decided to bring me to our province, Romblon.
